Databending (Old School Work)

Back

"Databending (or data bending) is the process of manipulating a media file of a certain format, using software designed to edit files of another format. Distortions in the medium typically occur as a result, and the process either falls under a broader category of, or is frequently employed in glitch art."

As part of my Photography A level I took these images on B&W 135 film, developed and digitised them, then edited them as if they were audio files using Audacity.

The images are of the dishes at RAF Stenigot, three of which have since been scraped. The images were exhibited at Gallery at St.Martin's.
